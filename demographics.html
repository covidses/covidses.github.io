Baseline Characteristics

Lab Results

Count, mean age, and standard deviation of individuals that received viral polymerase chain reaction (PCR) testing for SARS-CoV-2 in the MGB system between February 1, 2020 and July 25, 2020. All MGB employees and individuals under the age of 18 are excluded. Also includes the population stratified by test results, race, and primary language spoken.

Inpatients

Count, mean age, and standard deviation of individuals that tested positive for SARS-CoV-2. All MGB employees and individuals under the age of 18 are excluded. Also includes the population stratified by whether patient was hospitalized due to SARS-CoV-2, race, and primary language spoken.

DT::datatable(inpatient_table)

Death

Count, mean age, and standard deviation of individuals that were hospitilized due to SARS-CoV-2. All MGB employees and individuals under the age of 18 are excluded. Also includes the population stratified by whether patient died due to SARS-CoV-2, race, and primary language spoken.

DT::datatable(death_table)
